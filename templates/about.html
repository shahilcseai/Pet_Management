About Paw-Connect

Connecting loving families with furry companions since 2023.

Our mission is to create a world where every pet has a loving home and receives the care, respect, and compassion they deserve.

Our Story

How It All Began

Paw-Connect was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate animal lovers who recognized the need for a better way to connect adoptable pets with loving homes.

After volunteering at various animal shelters, our founders noticed that many amazing pets were overlooked simply because they didn't have enough exposure or the right platform to showcase their unique personalities.

With backgrounds in technology, animal welfare, and community organizing, they joined forces to create Paw-Connect - a comprehensive platform that not only helps pets find homes but also supports pet owners with resources, products, and a supportive community.

What started as a small local initiative has grown into a thriving community of pet lovers, dedicated to improving the lives of animals everywhere.

Our Mission & Values

Our Mission

At Paw-Connect, our mission is to revolutionize the pet adoption process by creating meaningful connections between pets and their future families, while providing education and resources to ensure successful, lifelong relationships.

We strive to:

  • Find loving homes for animals in need
  • Reduce pet homelessness and shelter overcrowding
  • Support pet owners with quality products and resources
  • Promote responsible pet ownership through education
  • Create a compassionate community of pet lovers
